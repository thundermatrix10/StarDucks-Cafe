52 Years of Serving Coffees

Founded in 1976, StarDuck Cafè has been a pioneer in bringing exceptional coffee experiences to our community. What started as a small, family-owned café has grown into a beloved delivery service, dedicated to providing freshly brewed coffee to your doorstep. Our journey from a cozy neighborhood spot to a leading coffee delivery service is fueled by our passion for quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction. With decades of experience and a commitment to excellence, StarDucks Cafè ensures that every cup of coffee we deliver is crafted with care and delivered with love. Join us in celebrating over four decades of brewing happiness, one cup at a time.
